Design

The Range Rover Velar epitomizes modern elegance and cutting-edge design, striking a perfect balance between sophisticated minimalism and advanced functionality. Its sleek, streamlined silhouette is complemented by flush door handles, a floating roof, and premium matrix LED headlights, delivering an unmistakable presence on the road. Inside, the Velar redefines luxury with a contemporary, clutter-free cabin featuring a dual-touchscreen interface, premium materials, and customizable ambient lighting.

Every detail, from the seamless integration of technology to the ergonomic seating, has been crafted to provide an unparalleled driving experience while embodying Range Rover’s heritage of refinement and innovation.

Technology

The Range Rover Velar seamlessly blends cutting-edge hybrid technology with its iconic design and modern functionality. Offering a plug-in hybrid (PHEV) option, the Velar delivers an efficient driving experience with lower emissions and the ability to drive up to 53 km (33 miles) in pure electric mode—ideal for city commuting or short trips. Its advanced powertrain combines a 2.0-liter petrol engine with an electric motor to deliver an impressive fuel efficiency of just 2.3 liters per 100 km (WLTP combined), ensuring a perfect balance between performance and sustainability.

The hybrid system also includes regenerative braking, which recharges the battery while driving, enhancing overall energy efficiency. This harmonious blend of luxury and eco-conscious engineering sets the Velar apart in its class.

Luxury

The Range Rover Velar defines modern luxury, combining refined craftsmanship with premium materials and an atmosphere of unparalleled comfort. The meticulously designed cabin offers luxurious seating, featuring high-quality leather, soft-touch surfaces, and customizable ambient lighting to set the perfect mood for any journey. Elegant details like polished chrome accents, sophisticated wood veneers, and a floating center console enhance the Velar's minimalist yet opulent aesthetic.

Its spacious interior ensures comfort for every passenger, while innovative features like dual touchscreens, Meridian™ sound systems, and advanced climate control transform every drive into an indulgent experience. The Range Rover Velar embodies the epitome of luxury, delivering a sanctuary of tranquility and style.

Trim

The Range Rover Velar is available in multiple trims with varying price points in Morocco, each tailored to different levels of luxury and performance:
1. Velar Standard: Starting around 658,000 MAD, offering a robust entry into the lineup with premium features.
2. Dynamic SE: Priced approximately at 710,000 MAD, with enhanced styling and additional technology.
3. Dynamic HSE: Starting from 800,000 MAD, including more advanced luxury and driver assistance features.
4. Autobiography: The top-tier version with the most refined options, available at approximately 965,000 MAD. These prices are estimates based on configurations and may vary depending on customizations and regional factors.
